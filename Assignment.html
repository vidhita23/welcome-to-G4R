Trademark Assignment/
Transmission

Assignment of a trademark means the transfer of the owner’s right, title, and interest with respect to the trademark. Assignment is defined as an assignment in writing by act of the parties concerned. The assignment of a trademark occurs when there is a transfer of the owner’s right, title, and interest with respect to the trademark. In another way, we can say it is the transfer of ownership with or without the goodwill of the business. In case the trademark is registered, such an assignment has to be recorded in the Register of Trademark. One can assign a trademark through a trademark assignment agreement

“Transmission” means the transmission by operation of law and any other mode of transfer, not being assignment.

There are different ways to assign or transfer a trademark in India

  • Complete Assignment to another entity
  • Partial Assignment
  • Assignment With Goodwill
  • Assignment Without Goodwill
